Coding Brum

About Us

Coding Brum is a coding club for young people under 18, which takes place in the public space at the BBC in the Mailbox in Birmingham City Centre. We cater for coders of all abilities from beginners to experts, and we welcome anybody who wishes to learn how to code.

Coding Brum is free for all to attend, however participants will have to bring their own hardware or software which they wish you use - if you have a coding-related project, that you wish to demonstrate, please contact us here!.
